HIKING IN BANDARBAN

Keokradong: This is the highest peak in Bangladesh, and the views from the top are simply breathtaking. The hike is about 3-4 hours long, and it is moderately challenging.


Nilgiri:This is a popular spot for hiking, as it offers a variety of trails to choose from, ranging from easy to challenging. There are also several waterfalls and lakes in the area, making it a great place to explore.

Chimbuk Hill: This is a short and easy hike that is perfect for families. The views from the top are not as spectacular as Keokradong, but it is still a great way to enjoy the fresh air and scenery.

Nafa-khum: This is a challenging hike that takes you through dense forests and over steep terrain. The views from the top are worth the effort, as you can see for miles in every direction.

IKING IN KHAGRACHARI

Nunchuri Jorna:This is a moderate 3-4 hour hike that takes you through lush forests and over cascading waterfalls. The trail starts from the Nunchuri village and ends at the Jorna waterfall.

Putongti Hill:This is a challenging 5-6 hour hike that offers stunning views of the surrounding hills and valleys. The trail starts from the Putongti village and ends at the summit of the hill.

Mairung Valley: This is an easy 2-3 hour hike that takes you through a beautiful valley with rice paddies, tea gardens, and forests. The trail starts from the Mairung village and ends at the Mairung waterfall. more info
